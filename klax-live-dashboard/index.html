Temperature Bucket Probabilities

Probability of the daily high falling in each 2°F bucket. The daily high is the maximum temperature across all forecast hours within the NWS climate day (midnight-to-midnight Pacific Time). It uses the near-resolution-adjusted blended forecast (HRRR 60% + ECMWF 40%) when live METAR obs are available, falling back to the blended forecast otherwise. The high can occur at any hour — including early morning before a cold front. Sigma grows with the forecast hour of the high (±0.8°F at hour 0, ±5.5°F at hour 48+). Use the dropdown to look back at what each forecast cycle predicted. This mimics the Polymarket multi-bucket market structure.
